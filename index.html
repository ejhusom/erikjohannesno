About

I work in research, focusing on applied machine learning (more details below). I have a MSc degree in Computational Science from the University of Oslo, Norway. I love to be in the mountains, and spend most of my spare time running and skiing off the beaten track. I also play piano and church organ.

This site has no tracking and no comment section. If you want to contact me with feedback, comments, questions or hate mail, don't hesitate to send me an e-mail at erikjohannes@protonmail.com.
